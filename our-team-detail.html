Dr Abdul Matin Azizi

GDC 264558
Principal Dentist

BSc, BDS

Dr Azizi graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Biomedical Sciences before going onto to receive his Bachelor’s of Dental Surgery from Plymouth University

Since then, he has continued to further his study and skillset by completing numerous postgraduate courses with world class leaders in their field.

These include cosmetic, restorative, and aesthetic dentistry. Dedicated to providing the top general dentistry to his patients, Dr Azizi is also interested in cosmetic dentistry, especially ceramic veneers and composite bonding. Having trained with world leading cosmetic doctors on Harley Street, he is also passionate about facial aesthetics. Other areas of interest include Invisalign® and full mouth rehabilitation.
